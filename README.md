### 1. **banking_analytics/**

### 2. **claims_data_reconciliation/**
Automated reconciliation and validation of claims and payments across multiple data sources.  
**Tech:** Python, Pandas, Excel  
**Focus:** Data quality control, exception flagging, and audit-ready reporting.

---

### 3. **credit_decision_system/**
End-to-end credit decision engine combining feature engineering, model scoring, and decision thresholds.  
**Tech:** Python (Scikit-Learn), Pandas, SHAP  
**Focus:** Credit scoring pipeline design, interpretability, and business rule integration.

---

### 4. **credit_risk_assessment/**
Credit risk modelling using the **German Credit Risk dataset** to estimate probability of default (PD).  
**Tech:** Python (Logistic Regression, Random Forest, Gradient Boosting), Pandas, SHAP  
**Focus:** Model governance, PD calibration, explainability, and stress testing.

---

### 5. **crude_forecast_var/**
Forecasting daily crude oil price volatility and computing a simplified Value-at-Risk metric.  
**Tech:** Python, NumPy, StatsModels  
**Focus:** Time-series modelling, volatility estimation, risk forecasting.

---

### 6. **customer_risk_pricing_model/**
Machine-learning model to price customer credit risk and predict expected returns under different risk bands.  
**Tech:** Python, Scikit-Learn, Pandas  
**Focus:** Predictive pricing models and risk-adjusted profitability analysis.

---

---

### 8. **stock_prediction/**
Stock movement forecasting using historical price data and technical indicators.  
**Tech:** Python, Scikit-Learn, yFinance, Matplotlib  
**Focus:** Feature engineering, regression/classification modelling, time-series evaluation.

---

### 9. **trading_workflow_automation/**
Python system automating post-trade reconciliation and exception reporting.  
**Tech:** Python, Pandas, SQL  
**Focus:** Middle-office workflows, trade validation, reconciliation logic, and audit logs.

### 10. **transaction_anomaly_detector/**
Dashboard for detecting anomalies, chargebacks, and refunds in transaction data.
